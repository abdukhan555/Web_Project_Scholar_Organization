About OIST Internship Program?

Every year Okinawa Institute of Science and Technology, Japan invites International talented students to participate in different research projects which revolutionary change world history. International students from all over the world are eligible to apply for OIST paid internship program 2022 in Japan. OIST Internship is a Research Internship 2022 program in Japan.

OIST International interns who have outstanding educational backgrounds will do research in the field of science and technology. The OIST Graduate School, Japan Internships are offered for research opportunities to undergraduate (BS/Bachelors Students) and postgraduate (MS / MPhil / Masters Students) students as research interns. All the Selected OIST Students will work under the OIST teachers & professors and contribute to the different research activities of the OIST, Japan Japan

OIST is a Fully Funded Internship for International students from all over the world. As it is Fully Funded Paid Internship Japan, all the Costs during the Internship period will be cover by the OIST. For students who are Graduate or Undergraduate enrolled students, it does not matter as you can apply for this program from any part of the world. There is no need for any IELTS/TOEFL for Japan Internship Program 2022 under OIST.  There are no application fees for apply, don't miss this opportunity in japan & enjoy your quality time in Japan.

What are the Eligibility Criteria for OIST Japan Internship?

All the applicants must have to fulfill this criterion in order to select for the 2022 Japan OIST Internship.

Eligible Countries:  Students of any nationality are eligible to apply.

Other Requirements:

  1. Students currently enrolled in undergraduate or Masters's Degree programs in worldwide universities, colleges, junior colleges, training institutes, and vocational schools in Japan or overseas, or recent graduates of such institutions are eligible to apply.
  2. Presently studying (enrolled) students must obtain an approval letter from their home institution.
  3. The student's academic background must match and be appropriate to the host unit (s) according to the OIST requirements.
  4. If you are a Recent Graduate student, you are also eligible to apply.
  5. Graduate School funded internships are highly competitive and are only granted to international students with an excellent academic record who is in the final 2 years of their academic program

The OIST Internships 2022 Applications are Opens, the last date to apply is 15th October 2021. The internship will start Between April 1st- September 30th. The Result of the Internship will be Announced in January
