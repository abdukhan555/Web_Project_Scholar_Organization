Swiss Government Excellence Scholarships 2022 | Fully Funded:

The Swiss Government Excellence Scholarships 2022-2023 are a key component of the Swiss Government's worldwide education, research, and innovation program. Master's, Ph.D., and Post-Doctoral students from across the globe can apply for fully-funded scholarships in Switzerland. However, the research stipend is only offered to aspiring and extremely self-motivated Ph.D. and Post-Doc students, and the international Swiss Government Scholarship is only open to students with a master's degree. Students that are inquisitive, have fantastic ideas, have an open-mindedness, and are keen on cultural interaction should apply for this highly demanding scholarship program.

The Swiss Confederation offers Government Excellence Scholarships every year to encourage international collaboration and exchange via research and the arts between Switzerland and more than 180 countries around the world. These international scholarships in Europe are awarded to citizens of countries with whom Switzerland holds diplomatic ties. The Swiss Government Excellence Scholarships also boost Switzerland's international reputation as a leading ERI player and in supporting research culture.

What are the benefits of Swiss Government Excellence Scholarships:

There are two types of scholarships available under the Swiss Government Excellence Scholarship for international students for Study in Switzerland.

Research Scholarship program:
Under this program, applicants can apply in any field of study, and research scholars can study at any Swiss cantonal university, university of applied sciences, two federal institutes of technology, and four research institutes.
Art Scholarship program:
Art scholarships are available to students pursuing a master's degree in the field of art. This group is restricted to a small number of nations. Holders of an art scholarship can attend any Swiss conservatory or university of the arts.

Expenses that will cover by Swiss Government Excellence Scholarships:

  1. Master's, Ph.D., and postdoctoral students will receive a monthly stipend.
  2. There is no charge for the application procedure.
  3. Swiss health insurance will be given as a requirement.
  4. After completing their studies, students from non-EU and non-EFTA nations will get a travel allowance (return to their home country).
  5. At the start of the scholarship, a special housing allowance of 300 CHF will be provided.
  6. Public transportation will be half-priced for students.
  7. Support services, excursions, meals, and sightseeing tours will be available to students.

Eligibility Criteria for Swiss Government Excellence Scholarships:

In order to qualify for a place on the program you will need to meet the following requirements:

  1. To be considered for this award, the candidate must obtain a letter of acceptance from a Swiss university professor. As a result, you must understand how to obtain a professor's acceptance letter.
  2. A master's degree equal to a Swiss master's degree is required for Ph.D. and Post-doctoral positions.
  3. A bachelor's degree comparable to a Swiss bachelor's degree is required for admission to the master's program.
  4. The applicant must be a citizen of a country with which Switzerland maintains diplomatic ties.
  5. Scholarship recipients must be able to relocate to Switzerland for the duration of their studies.
  6. Applicants who have spent more than a year in Switzerland are ineligible to apply.
  7. Any candidate who has previously received a Swiss government grant is unable to apply.
  8. The master's program eligibility varies somewhat from nation to country. Select Your Country: Countries A – L / Countries M – Z to see whether you meet the qualifying requirements.

Documents Required for Swiss Government Excellence Scholarships:

  1. A concise cv is attached.
  2. A motivational letter.
  3. Using the FCS form, create a research plan of no more than 5 pages (signed).
  4. Two sets of documents should be submitted: one original and one copy.
  5. The original signed documents, as well as two letters of recommendation, must be included in the first set (if applicable attested in English).
  6. Except for recommendation letters, the second set must contain a duplicate of the previous set.
  7. A photo-enhanced FCS form that has been written and printed.

What is the Deadline for Swiss Government Excellence Scholarships 2022?

The deadline for submitting an application varies by country. The outcome of the Swiss scholarship will be revealed by the end of May 2022

How to Apply for Swiss Government Excellence Scholarships?

  1. Applicants must apply individually to Swiss universities for Ph.D., Post-Doctoral, or Master's degrees, as well as to the Federal Commission of Scholarships for Foreign Students (FCS).
  2. The candidate must provide a full profile as well as a research project or artistic piece.
  3. The candidate must locate a supervisor from a selected Swiss university on his or her own.
  4. It's best if the research of the professors you choose is related to your field of study.
  5. Make direct touch with your lecturer and let them know you're interested in a Swiss Government scholarship.
  6. Students wishing to apply for Arts must first establish their country's eligibility.
  7. It is required that the paper be written in English.
