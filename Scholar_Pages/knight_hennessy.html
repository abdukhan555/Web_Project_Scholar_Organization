Eligibility Criteria for Knight Hennessy Scholarship:

Candidates must fulfill the following criteria to avail of this Scholarship for their study in USA.

  1. Applicants from all over the world are accepted
  2. Applicants must be healthy both physically and mentally, excellent in academic standing, and with good conduct.
  3. Students must have a Bachelors's Degree in Any Discipline.
  4. If you have completed your bachelor’s Degree or Master’s Degree before 2015, you are not eligible.
  5. Students who are still in college studying for their first/bachelor’s degree, are eligible to apply as long as their first/bachelor’s degrees are not complete before they enroll as a graduate student at Stanford.
  6. Students who earned a graduate degree, remain eligible to apply in 2021 as long as they earned their first/bachelor’s degree in 2015 or later.
  7. Students earned or expect to earn a degree from a university where English is the official and sole language of instruction, then you do not need results of an English proficiency exam.
  8. English Language Requirements, GRE, IELTS, TOEFL, GMAT, etc.

Meet the Scholars of Knight Hennessy Scholarship:

We are delighted to introduce the 2021 cohort of Knight-Hennessy Scholars. The scholars in this cohort hold passports from 26 different countries. They earned undergraduate degrees at 16 international and 34 U.S. institutions.  Eighteen percent were the first members of their families to attend college, and 8 percent serve in the US military. As a cohort, they will pursue degrees in 39 programs at Stanford.
