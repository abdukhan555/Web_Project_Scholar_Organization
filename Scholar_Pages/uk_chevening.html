  3. If you have been called for an interview then in the interview you can let them know about the 4th University in which you have an admission offer.

Important Note: Students do not need to have a University admission letter to apply for the Chevening Scholarship at this point.

  1. Give preference to Universities in your application which you feel is the brightest chances of getting admission to one of the Best UK universities.

What are the Eligibility Criteria for the Chevening Scholarship?

Applicants must fulfill the following eligibility criteria to avail of this UK Government Scholarship Program:

  1. There are about 160+ Eligible Countries for Chevening Scholarship 2022 Around the World. Please check your country: List of Eligible Countries

Eligible types of Work Experience:

The student must ensure that he/she meets the minimum work experience requirement for the scholarship before submitting your Chevening application. Chevening Scholarships require that applicants have at least two years of work experience.

The types of work experience that are eligible for Chevening can include:

  1.     Full-time employment
  2.     Part-time employment
  3.     Voluntary work
  4.     Paid or unpaid internships

For more information about the work experience requirements: Please Visit Here

Other Requirements:

  1. Applicant must be a citizen/national of Eligible Chevening countries or territory.
  2. The applicant must be returned back to his/her home country after the completion of your award has ended.
  3. English Proficiency Requirement
  4. Have completed all components of an undergraduate degree that will enable you to gain entry onto a postgraduate program at a UK university by the time you submit your application. This is typically equivalent to an upper second-class 2:1 honors degree in the UK.
  5. Have at least two years of work experience.
  6. Apply to three different eligible UK Universities Courses and have received an unconditional offer from one of these choices.

What Documents are Required for Apply:

 The list of documents is given below:

  1.     Online Application
  2.     Transcripts/Degree
  3.     Research proposal/ Study Plan
  4.     Recommendation Letters
