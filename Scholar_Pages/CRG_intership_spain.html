About Centre for Genomic Regulation:

The CRG (Centre for Genomic Regulation) is one of the top biomedical and genomics research center based on Barcelona, Spain. The laboratories and its research facilities are located in the Barcelona, Spain Biomedical Research Park, in front of Somorrostro beach.

Ready for Internship in Europe? International Internships in Spain is open now for international students. The CRG is finally launches the 9th Summer Internship 2021 program that will be offered to undergraduate  and master students to conduct summer research at the CRG. The goal of the program is to encourage international students, from any nationality around the globe, in the pursuit of a scientific career and to introduce them to an international laboratory experience.

What is the Eligibility Criteria for CRG Summer Internship:

Eligibility criteria for undergraduates:

  1. Undergraduates \ Bachelors Student with an excellent academic record and must have a strong interest in biosciences and biomedical research (prior lab experience is highly recommended if available) who are in their final two years of studies in the Life Sciences or related subjects (e.g. Bioinformatics, Biomedicine, Biochemistry, Pharmacology, Chemistry).
  2. Two full years (4 semesters) of college course work completed by the time the internship commences.
  3. Candidates may not have received an undergraduate degree at the time of the internship (Summer 2021).
  4. Candidates may not have previously obtained a “CRG Summer Internship”.
  5. High level of English.
  6. You have the availability to do a student placement/internship agreement through your University

Eligibility criteria for master students:

  1. Master students with an excellent academic record and a strong interest in biosciences and biomedical research (prior lab experience is highly recommended) who are currently enrolled in a master's in an accredited university and after the summer internship.
  2. Candidates may not have previously obtained a “CRG Summer Internship”
  3. High level of English.
  4. You have the availability to do a student placement/internship agreement through your University.
  5. Candidates may not have been at the CRG before the internship starts.

Application Timeline for CRG Summer Internship:

  1. Call opening 15th  -   January 2021
  2. Call deadline  -  8th March 2021
  3. Pre-selection of candidates  -  March
  4. Notification of candidates  -  End of March
  5. Start of the internship  -  From June/July 2021
  6. End of the internship  -  September 2021
